PROJECTS


AI Skin Expert

AI Skin Expert is a mobile-based web application designed to provide accurate and efficientprediction of skin diseases. With the increasing complexity and uncertainty in dermatology,traditional diagnosis methods often require extensive laboratory tests and expertconsultation. Our proposed system aims to revolutionize this process by leveragingcomputer vision and symptom-based prediction models. The system offers two mainfunctionalities: image-based disease prediction and symptom-based disease prediction. Users can either upload a photo of the affected area or input their symptoms for analysis. The application then utilizes convolutional neural networks (CNNs) for image analysis andadvanced algorithms for symptom-based prediction, providing users with quick and reliableresults. By offering a user-friendly interface and leveraging state-of-the-art AI techniques, AISkin Expert aims to improve accessibility to dermatological diagnosis, leading to earlierdetection and treatment of skin abnormalities. This project holds great potential in enhancingoverall well-being by addressing the challenges posed by skin diseases and reducing therisks associated with delayed diagnosis andtreatment
